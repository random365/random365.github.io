Data Visualization Project - Gender Inequality

Data source: OECD - https://data.oecd.org/earnwage/gender-wage-gap.htm

The gender wage gap as used here is the difference in median earnings between men and women relative to the median earnings of men
The Mean Wage Gap for a given year is the sum of the individual wage gaps of the countries providing data for that year, divided by the number of countries
